#define FRAMES_BUFFER_LEN   65536

Calculates the histogram of an audio sample and corresponding entropy. Calculates the histogram of the left and right channels, as well as the histogram of the average of the channels (the mono version). For visualizing graphically the histograms, it is used the matplotlib library.
